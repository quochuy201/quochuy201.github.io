Customer Review Analysis and Text Mining

With the development of the Internet, technology and e-commerce, online-purchasing is easier and more convenient these days. Online reviews become the main source of information that customers usually refer to for their purchasing decisions. However, many reviews given by online customers are not considered truthfuly. Because of commercial benefits, fake reviews are generated to mislead customers. Therefore, it is necessary to detect fake reviews effectively.

This paper aims to improve the performance of fake review classifiers by integrating different techniques into classifying models. More specifically, I analyzed similarities between reviews and utilized the EM (Expectation-Maximization) clustering algorithm to recognize the review patterns. I also applied sentiment analysis to analyze the reviews. Using the results from clustering models, sentiment analysis, and non-textual features of reviews and reviewers, we built machine learning models to classify fake reviews.

Language and technologies I used: Python, nltk, scikit-learn, SSIS, AWS: RDS and E2C.

Aireport and Airline Choice

This is a Customer Behavior Prediction Problem. There are two international airports in Seoul Metropolitan Area: Gimpo Airport - Small, old, close to the city and Incheon Airport - Lager, new, modern, but far from the city. What drives passengers' choice of airport and airline choice? In this project, our objectives are to investigate passengers' choice behavior, apply several different approaches to modeling air travel behavior, and discuss substantive policy implications based on our quantitative analysis and predictive model.
